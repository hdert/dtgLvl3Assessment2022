What Lies Beneath

An exhibition of photographic composite works that challenge the perceptions of reality.

As a traveling exhibition, 'What Lies Beneath' is an inspirational showcase of altered reality coming to a town near you. Find a location close to you from the list below, and mark the date and place in your diary. You won't be disappointed when you immerse yourself in the fantastical, creative works of the exhibiting artists: Jack Pott, Kay Oss, Barry D. Hatchett and Anita Room.
